Public relations

Public relations is aimed at creating and maintaining a positive image of a brand among the public, potential customers, and other stakeholders. Branding is the main component of PR: it is important to create a positive public perception of the product or person.

By creating a comprehensive image of your company, HR-brand, or product, we make your brand recognizable and trustworthy to potential customers.

Stages of public relations:

  1. As a first step, we create a plan for your business, create a calendar of updates, integrate new features, and add new partners. During this stage, we also assess your brand awareness and visibility.
  2. Secondly, we set measurable goals. In order to accomplish your goal, strategies include methods of communication, messages conveyed, and other activities. Since you are likely to have several strategies for achieving your objectives, some strategies may serve several purposes.
  3. Our next step is to create a PR schedule and contact bloggers and media to make the first press releases and reviews of your project. Once your press release is approved, it will be placed on the newswire and your campaign will begin! In order to reach the media, our team will load up different pitch letters. Besides calling the media, we will email them and send all necessary information about your project to get you the coverage you need.
